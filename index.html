About Me

Laboni Akter

I am a professional nutritionist with a Bachelor's and ongoing Master's degree in Nutrition and Food Science from the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science, University of Dhaka. Currently, I'm working as a nutritionist at American Wellness Centre (AWC).

My goal is to make your life beautiful and bring new wellness through science-based nutrition advice.

  • Bachelor's & Master's in Nutrition and Food Science
  • Professional Nutritionist at American Wellness Centre
  • Specialized in various health conditions
  • Personalized nutrition plans for each patient

I believe that proper nutrition is not just about curing diseases, but also about improving the quality of life. My main goal is to provide personalized, scientific and effective nutrition solutions for each patient.
